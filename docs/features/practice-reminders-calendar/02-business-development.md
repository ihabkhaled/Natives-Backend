# Business development and commercial impact

The feature improves attendance reliability and member trust without adding a paid vendor. It
supports all team accounts and has no contract or billing change. Existing in-app delivery remains
available while calendar feeds work in standard calendar clients.

Adoption risks are leaked feed links, unclear quiet-hour behavior, and users expecting private
practice notes in calendar entries. Enablement must state that feed URLs are bearer credentials,
can be revoked, and intentionally omit private notes and participant data.

Rollout audience: internal QA team, then selected team administrators, then all teams after security
and calendar-client compatibility checks.
